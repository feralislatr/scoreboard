Agile Methodology


Agile implementation : Are teams using an agile methodology to guide their SDLC?
Scrum: do teams integrate regular sprints (could be daily or every 2 hours) and daily deliverables?
Estimation : do teams predefine the features/story-points that should be delivered upon at the end of the day?
Tracking: do teams use tracking software to determine if they are on track?
Design Phase : do teams spend a core amount of time designing their project before the implementation phase? Do teams iterate through daily design phases? Or is design over-looked?
